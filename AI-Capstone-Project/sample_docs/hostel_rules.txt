UNIVERSITY HOSTEL RULES AND REGULATIONS 2024-2025

SECTION 1: HOSTEL ACCOMMODATION

1.1 Eligibility
Hostel accommodation is available to all full-time students of the university. Priority is given to:
1. Students from outside the city (more than 50 km from campus)
2. First-year students
3. Students with disabilities
4. Female students (separate hostels available)

1.2 Allotment Procedure
Room allotment is done by the Hostel Warden based on seniority, availability, and medical requirements. Students cannot choose their own rooms. Requests for room changes will be considered after one full semester.

1.3 Hostel Facilities
Each hostel block is equipped with:
- 24/7 Wi-Fi connectivity (20 Mbps per room)
- Common room with TV and indoor games
- Laundry facility (washing machines)
- Reading room (open till midnight)
- Gym facility (6 AM to 9 PM)
- Solar-powered hot water supply
- Backup power (generator) during outages

SECTION 2: DISCIPLINE AND CONDUCT

2.1 Prohibited Activities
The following are strictly prohibited in hostel premises:
- Consumption of alcohol, tobacco, or any narcotics
- Possession of firearms, sharp weapons, or explosives
- Ragging in any form (zero tolerance policy)
- Cooking in rooms (electric heaters, induction cooktops banned)
- Playing loud music after 10:00 PM
- Gambling in any form
- Keeping pets

2.2 Guest Policy
- Male guests are NOT permitted in female hostels and vice versa under any circumstances
- Guests may be entertained only in the common visiting room between 9:00 AM and 7:00 PM
- Overnight guests are strictly prohibited
- Parents/guardians may visit during weekends with prior intimation to warden

2.3 Ragging
Any act of ragging is a cognizable offence under the law. Complaints can be filed at:
- Anti-Ragging Cell: +91-XXXX-XXXXXX (24/7 helpline)
- University Email: antiragging@university.edu.in
- UGC Helpline: 1800-180-5522

SECTION 3: TIMINGS AND MOVEMENT

3.1 Curfew Timings
- Weekdays (Monday–Friday): Return to hostel by 9:30 PM
- Weekends (Saturday–Sunday): Return by 10:30 PM
- Special events (prior permission needed): Up to 11:30 PM

3.2 Late Night Permission
For emergencies or special occasions, late night permission may be granted by the Chief Warden. This must be requested at least 24 hours in advance.

3.3 Outpass Policy
- Local outpass (within city): Valid for same day, must return by curfew time
- Overnight outpass: Requires written consent of parent/guardian
- Weekly outpass: Maximum 2 per month for undergraduate students

SECTION 4: MAINTENANCE AND CARE

4.1 Room Inspection
Monthly inspection is conducted by the warden's team. Rooms found in unhygienic conditions will attract a fine of Rs. 500.

4.2 Damage to Property
Students are responsible for the furniture, fittings, and equipment in their rooms. Damage must be reported immediately. Willful damage will result in cost recovery plus a penalty of 2x the damage cost.

4.3 Electricity Usage
Air conditioners are not permitted in standard rooms. Laptops, phones, and study lamps are permitted. Unauthorized use of high-power appliances will result in confiscation.

SECTION 5: COMPLAINTS AND GRIEVANCES

Students may register complaints through:
- Hostel Complaint Register (at warden's office)
- Online portal: university.edu.in/hostel/complaints
- Student Welfare Officer (SWO)

All genuine complaints will be addressed within 7 working days.
