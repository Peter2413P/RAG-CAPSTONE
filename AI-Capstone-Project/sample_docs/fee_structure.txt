UNIVERSITY FEE STRUCTURE AND PAYMENT GUIDELINES 2024-2025

SECTION A: TUITION FEES

A.1 Undergraduate Programs (Per Semester)
- B.Tech (All branches): Rs. 75,000
- B.Sc (Science streams): Rs. 35,000
- B.Com / BBA: Rs. 30,000
- B.A. (Humanities): Rs. 25,000

A.2 Postgraduate Programs (Per Semester)
- M.Tech (All branches): Rs. 55,000
- MBA: Rs. 65,000
- M.Sc: Rs. 30,000
- M.A.: Rs. 20,000

A.3 Research Programs (Annual)
- PhD (Full-time): Rs. 40,000
- PhD (Part-time): Rs. 25,000

SECTION B: OTHER MANDATORY FEES (ANNUAL)

- University Development Fund: Rs. 5,000
- Library Fee: Rs. 2,000
- Sports and Cultural Fee: Rs. 1,500
- Student Welfare Fund: Rs. 1,000
- Examination Fee (per semester): Rs. 1,200
- Identity Card and Smart Card: Rs. 500 (first year only)
- Laboratory Fee (for science/engineering students): Rs. 3,000

SECTION C: HOSTEL FEES

C.1 On-Campus Hostel (Per Semester)
- Double occupancy room: Rs. 18,000
- Triple occupancy room: Rs. 14,000
- Single occupancy room (limited): Rs. 25,000

C.2 Hostel Mess Charges
- Compulsory mess: Rs. 12,000 per semester
- Mess advance to be paid at the time of admission: Rs. 5,000

C.3 Hostel Deposit (Refundable)
- Security deposit: Rs. 10,000 (refunded upon vacating)

SECTION D: PAYMENT PROCEDURE

D.1 Payment Deadlines
- Semester fees must be paid within 30 days of semester commencement
- Late payment attracts a fine of Rs. 100 per day
- Maximum fine capped at Rs. 3,000

D.2 Payment Modes
Fees can be paid through:
1. Online portal (www.university.edu.in/fees) using Net Banking, UPI, Debit/Credit card
2. Demand Draft in favour of "The Registrar, University"
3. Cash payment at the Accounts Section (only up to Rs. 2,000)

D.3 Fee Receipts
Official fee receipts are generated only through the online portal. Students must preserve all fee receipts for the entire duration of their program.

SECTION E: SCHOLARSHIPS AND WAIVERS

E.1 Merit Scholarship
Students securing 9.0 CGPA and above are eligible for a 25% tuition fee waiver in the subsequent semester.

E.2 Government Scholarships
Students from economically weaker sections (EWS) and SC/ST/OBC categories are encouraged to apply for government scholarship schemes:
- Post-Matric Scholarship: Covers full tuition for eligible students
- Central Sector Scholarship: Up to Rs. 20,000 per annum

E.3 Sports Quota Fee Concession
Students admitted under sports quota receive a 50% fee waiver on tuition fees for the entire duration of the program.

SECTION F: REFUND POLICY

F.1 Withdrawal Before Commencement of Classes
- Full refund minus Rs. 1,000 processing fee

F.2 Withdrawal Within 15 Days of Commencement
- 80% refund of tuition fee

F.3 Withdrawal After 15 Days
- No refund of tuition fee
- Hostel security deposit refunded in full after deducting damages if any
