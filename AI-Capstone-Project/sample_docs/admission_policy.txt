UNIVERSITY ADMISSION POLICY AND PROCEDURES 2024-2025

SECTION 1: UNDERGRADUATE ADMISSIONS

1.1 B.Tech Admission Criteria
Admission to B.Tech programs is based on:
- JEE Main score (70% weightage)
- Class 12 Board marks (30% weightage)
- Minimum eligibility: 60% in Class 12 with Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics

1.2 Lateral Entry (B.Tech 2nd Year)
- Diploma holders with 60% aggregate are eligible
- Admission through LECET/state-level entrance test
- Available seats: 20% of total intake (supernumerary)

1.3 B.Sc / B.A / B.Com Admission
- Based on Class 12 marks directly (merit list)
- No entrance test required
- Cut-off varies by stream and category

SECTION 2: POSTGRADUATE ADMISSIONS

2.1 M.Tech Admission
- GATE score mandatory for regular M.Tech
- Sponsored candidates: Relevant industry experience certificate required
- Minimum: B.Tech with 60% aggregate or equivalent CGPA

2.2 MBA Admission
- CAT/MAT/XAT score required
- Group Discussion and Personal Interview: 40% weightage
- Minimum: Bachelor's degree with 55% marks in any discipline

2.3 M.Sc Admission
- University-level entrance test (UGET)
- Minimum: B.Sc with 55% in relevant subject

SECTION 3: PHD ADMISSIONS

3.1 Eligibility
- M.Tech/M.E. with 60% marks: Direct PhD admission
- M.Sc/MA/MBA with 55% marks: PhD admission with coursework
- UGC-NET/CSIR-NET/GATE qualified candidates: Exempted from university entrance test

3.2 Procedure
1. Application through university portal
2. Written test (Research Aptitude + Subject Knowledge)
3. Interview with Doctoral Committee
4. Allotment of supervisor

3.3 Stipend for Full-time PhD Scholars
- GATE/NET qualified: Rs. 31,000/month (as per UGC norms)
- University fellowship: Rs. 15,000/month (limited seats)

SECTION 4: RESERVATION POLICY

4.1 Category-wise Seat Distribution
As per Government of India norms:
- SC: 15% of total seats
- ST: 7.5% of total seats
- OBC (Non-Creamy Layer): 27% of total seats
- EWS: 10% of total seats
- Unreserved/General: 40.5% of total seats
- PwD (horizontal reservation): 5% across all categories
- NRI/Foreign Nationals: Supernumerary seats (10% over intake)

4.2 Sports Quota
- 5% of seats in each program reserved for sports quota
- National/State level achievement certificates required

SECTION 5: DOCUMENTS REQUIRED

For all admissions, the following documents are mandatory:
1. Mark sheets of all qualifying examinations (original + 2 photocopies)
2. Transfer Certificate from previous institution
3. Migration Certificate (if from another university)
4. Caste/Category certificate (if applicable) - issued by competent authority
5. Domicile certificate
6. Recent passport-size photographs (6 copies)
7. Medical fitness certificate from registered MBBS doctor
8. Anti-ragging undertaking (by student and parent)
9. Entrance exam scorecard (if applicable)
10. Aadhaar card / passport (identity proof)
