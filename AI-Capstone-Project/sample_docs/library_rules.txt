UNIVERSITY CENTRAL LIBRARY RULES AND SERVICES 2024-2025

ABOUT THE LIBRARY
The University Central Library houses over 1,50,000 books, 500+ journals, and access to 25+ digital databases. It is spread across 3 floors covering 15,000 sq.ft and is fully air-conditioned.

SECTION 1: LIBRARY MEMBERSHIP

1.1 Eligibility and Membership
All registered students, faculty, and staff are automatic members of the library. Membership is activated upon payment of library fee and issue of library card.

1.2 Library Card
- Each member receives one smart library card (RFID-enabled)
- Lost card replacement: Rs. 200 fine + reissue
- Card is non-transferable

SECTION 2: BORROWING RULES

2.1 Number of Books Allowed
- Undergraduate students: 4 books at a time
- Postgraduate students: 6 books at a time
- PhD scholars: 10 books at a time
- Faculty: 15 books at a time

2.2 Loan Period
- Textbooks: 14 days
- Reference books: Not for home lending (reading room only)
- Journals and periodicals: Not for home lending
- Overnight lending (special request): Available for exam period

2.3 Renewal
Books can be renewed twice unless reserved by another member. Renewal via:
- Online portal: library.university.edu.in
- Mobile app: UniLib App
- Counter service

2.4 Overdue Fine
- Rs. 2 per book per day after due date
- Maximum fine per book: Rs. 100
- Members with unpaid fines cannot borrow new books

SECTION 3: DIGITAL RESOURCES

3.1 Online Databases Available
- IEEE Xplore (Engineering & Technology journals)
- ACM Digital Library (Computer Science)
- Elsevier ScienceDirect (All disciplines)
- JSTOR (Humanities and Social Sciences)
- PubMed (Medical and Life Sciences)
- Scopus (Multidisciplinary citations)
- Web of Science (Research impact)
- NPTEL Video Lectures (Free access)

3.2 Access Procedure
All databases are accessible:
- On campus: Direct access via university network
- Off campus: VPN required (download VPN config from IT portal)
- Username and password: Same as university email credentials

3.3 E-Books and Thesis
- Institutional repository: thesis.university.edu.in (all PhD theses free)
- E-books: Over 50,000 titles via Springer, Wiley, and Cambridge online

SECTION 4: LIBRARY TIMINGS

Regular Days (Monday–Friday): 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM
Saturday: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM
Sunday: 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM
Examination Period: Extended to 11:00 PM on weekdays

Holidays: Library remains open on most public holidays except national holidays (Republic Day, Independence Day, Gandhi Jayanti)

SECTION 5: CONDUCT IN LIBRARY

5.1 Dos
- Maintain silence in reading areas
- Switch phones to silent mode
- Carry university ID card
- Reserve seats for maximum 30 minutes if leaving temporarily

5.2 Don'ts
- No food or beverages inside the library
- No loud conversations or phone calls
- No sleeping in reading areas
- No reservation of seats with personal belongings for extended periods
- No marking, writing, or tearing of library books

5.3 Violations and Penalties
- First offense: Written warning
- Second offense: Suspension of borrowing privileges for 1 month
- Damage to books: Cost of book + 50% penalty
- Theft of library material: Disciplinary action + fine

SECTION 6: SPECIAL SERVICES

6.1 Inter-Library Loan (ILL)
Books not available in university library can be borrowed from partner institutions. Request via library portal. Processing time: 5-7 working days.

6.2 Document Delivery Service
Research articles not accessible via subscribed databases can be requested. Available to PhD scholars and faculty only.

6.3 Current Awareness Service
Monthly email digest of new acquisitions and journal issues sent to registered users.

6.4 Research Support
- Literature search assistance
- Citation management (Mendeley, Zotero training sessions)
- Plagiarism check: Turnitin access for thesis and research papers

6.5 Reading Club
Monthly book discussions open to all students. Check notice board for schedule.
